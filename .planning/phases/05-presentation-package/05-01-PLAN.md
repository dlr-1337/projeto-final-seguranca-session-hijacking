<threat_model>
## Threats

| ID | Threat | Severity | Mitigation in this plan |
|----|--------|----------|-------------------------|
| T-05-01 | Required assignment sections are missing from slides. | high | Build slides from the DOC-01 checklist and verify required terms with `Select-String`. |
| T-05-02 | Slides expose raw cookie values or real-looking sensitive data. | high | Evidence checklist requires redaction and fake/local-only data. |
| T-05-03 | The mitigation is overstated as making any active copied cookie impossible to replay. | high | Include the bearer-token caveat and Phase 4 denial scope in slides and script. |
| T-05-04 | The deck becomes too text-heavy for 25 minutes. | medium | Target 10-14 slides and move details into speaker notes/checklist. |
| T-05-05 | The presentation relies on an online slide service. | medium | Use local `docs/presentation/` artifacts only. |

## Residual Risk

The exact visual polish can still improve in Plan 05-02, but the content and required evidence will be present after this plan.
</threat_model>
